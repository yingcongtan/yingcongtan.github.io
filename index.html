About me

Starting in Aug. 2022, I will work at the TIDLE Lab, University of Toronto as a post-doctoral fellow under the supervision of Dr. J. Christopher Beck.

Before that I worked as a post-doctoral fellow at Concordia University under the supervision of Dr. Daria Terekhov and Dr. Andrew Delong.

I obtained my Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering at Concordia University in 2021, and my Ph.D. thesis is on Learning Linear Programs: Inverse Optimization as a Form of Machine Learning.

I am interested in interdisciplinary research topics in Operations Research and Machine Learning. In particular, my research interests lie in inverse optimization and its applications to planning and scheduling problems.
